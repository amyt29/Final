Snack time!

Puck is very interesting with his choices of snacks and treats, he will eat anything given to him so give him treats with caution! Although he has a select few he will fight on a hill for if possible

Puck's Top Five Favorite Treats

  • Bananas
  • Strawberries
  • Lettuce that has been dip in any type of stew
  • Any soft chewable treat (I recommend the wellness treats- soft and easy to eat good for both puppies and older dogs!)
